为什么Ripple优于Bitcoin币 Why Ripple Is Better Than Bitcoin

Posted at Dec 25th, 2013 Category: Ripple

Why Ripple is better than Bitcoin

1.Ripple is a means of payment, it is much more efficient to deal than Bitcoin, which takes hours.

2.As an investment, Bitcoin is much too “expensive”, thus lost much flexibility

3.Ripple as a virtual currency is much more convenient than Bitcoin, one can easily buy and sell at Ripple’s website.

If you want to understand Ripple more, please visit http://ripple.com

为什么Ripple优于比特币

1.Ripple币作为一种支付手段，远比Bitcoin方便快捷；

2.Ripple币作为一种投资，比Bitcoin相对便宜，容易介入，而Bitcoin已经过于昂贵，丧失了不少灵活性；

3.Ripple作为一种虚拟货币，比Bitcoin方便很多，可以直接在Ripple的官网上方便地买入与出售。

如果你想更为深入地了解Ripple，请登录网站：http://ripple.com
